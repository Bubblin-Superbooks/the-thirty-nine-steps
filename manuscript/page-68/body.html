I have eyes like a hawk, but I could see nothing moving in the whole countryside. Last of all I looked into the blue May sky, and there I saw that which set my pulses racing....

Low down in the south a monoplane was climbing into the heavens. I was as certain as if I had been told that that aeroplane was looking for me, and that it did not belong to the police.
